Data Types :-  Classification of Data.

			1. Number  -  Only Numbers(Integers and Decimals)

Important Points :- 

1. NaN stands for "Not a Number", It is a special value that represents the result of an invalid or an unrepresentable mathematical operation. It is of the Number data type, despite its name, and is a global property that can also be accessed as Number.NaN.

2. undefined :-  It gives as value when you are not  providing initial value for a variable.
